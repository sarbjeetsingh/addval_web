kulcare


Category: A complete technology solution for a startup idea
Task: Build a healthcare platform for patients where they can effectively manage their health and connect with healthcare providers

kulcare is a healthcare marketplace where patients and healthcare providers connect with each other.

kulcare helps the patients to store their medical records, manage their medication and their health, take online consultation, order medicines and much more. It also allows patients to add their family and loved ones to take care of their health. It is an ongoing project, aiming to revolutionize the healthcare industry.

We have built a web app, separate mobile apps (iOS and Android) for patients, doctors, hospitals, labs which are connected together.

Fade
×

Fade

Client: Fade Inc
Task: Build a chatting platform that lets users chat in the most secure way possible

Fade is a chatting platform that was started with one prime focus - security and privacy.

It is a multi-platform chatting application that give you the complete control over the messages you sent. It has industry standard end-to-end encryption for your protection. You can choose to take back an already sent message. You can choose to or not to allow others to take a screenshot of your chat or copy it.

You can also send images, videos and audios with same confidence as chat messages.

Client: Tradeya Inc
Task: Build a platform for everyone to exchange goods and services in the modern world

Everyone has a lot of value in terms of products they own or skills they that they do not realise. And not everyone has the money to spend on the crazy wants.
